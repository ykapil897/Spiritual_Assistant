Mayavadis propose that the world we experience is false, or *mithya*. As Srila Prabhupada explains in Perfect Questions Perfect Answers, "The Mayavadis, they say that this world is false. *Brahman satyam jagan mithya*: 'The spirit soul is truth, and the world is false.'"
