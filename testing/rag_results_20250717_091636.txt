**The Caitya Guru: The Inner Guide**

In the pursuit of spiritual development, the role of the caitya guru, or the Supersoul, is of paramount importance. The caitya guru is the internal manifestation of the Supreme Lord, guiding the devotee from within.

**The Caitya Guru: The Supersoul Within**

As Srila Prabhupada explains, "The caitya-guru, the spiritual master within the heart, is Kṛṣṇa Himself." [Teachings of Lord Kapila 3, Purport](https://github.com/iskconpress/books/blob/master/tlk/3.txt/). This internal guide is the ultimate authority, directing the devotee on the path of self-realization.

**Guidance from Within**

The caitya guru not only guides the devotee from within but also receives guidance from the external spiritual master. As Srila Prabhupada states, "The caitya-guru, the spiritual master, is guiding him from within, and the devotee is getting help from without." [Teachings of Lord Kapila 12, Purport](https://github.com/iskconpress/books/blob/master/tlk/12.txt/). This harmonious relationship between the internal and external guides enables the devotee to make progress on the spiritual journey.

**The Ultimate Authority**

In this regard, Srila Prabhupada emphasizes, "The caitya-guru, or the spiritual master, is the external manifestation of the Supersoul, and He helps the devotee in consultation with the Supersoul." [Nectar of Devotion 1, Purport](https://github.com/iskconpress/books/nod/1.txt/). The caitya guru, as the ultimate authority, ensures that the devotee remains on the path of devotion, free from the influence of material desires.
