According to Srila Prabhupada, "His disciples and granddisciples are his subbranches" [CC Adi 10.16, Translation](https://github.com/iskconpress/books/tree/master/cc/adi/10/16.txt). Srila Prabhupada further notes that "to describe them all would be difficult" [CC Adi 10.16, Translation](https://github.com/iskconpress/books/tree/master/cc/adi/10/16.txt). **The Significance of Disciplic Succession**

In the context of Gaudiya Vaishnavism, the concept of disciplic succession is crucial, as it ensures the continuation of the spiritual master's teachings and legacy. Srila Prabhupada emphasizes the importance of this succession, stating, "One should not become a spiritual master for the sake of profit or reputation. One must be a spiritual master for the benefit of the disciples" [The Nectar of Instruction, Text 5](https://vedabase.io/en/library/the-nectar-of-instruction/text-5/).
