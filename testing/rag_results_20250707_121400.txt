**Core Teaching:**
In this chapter, we see the followers of Lord Śiva, led by Vīrabhadra, creating disturbances in the arena of sacrifice performed by Prajāpati Dakṣa. Vīrabhadra, the personified anger of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, was prepared to execute the orders of Lord Śiva (SB 4.5.5, Translation). He threw the head of Dakṣa into the sacrificial fire as an oblation, devastating the arrangements for sacrifice (SB 4.5.26, Translation).
